## Who We Are

AAi (Abrahamic Alliance International) unites Jews, Christians, and Muslims to collaborate in compassion. We believe that by working together to serve the poor, we can build bridges of understanding and respect between our communities.

## Our Vision

To see poverty alleviated and lives saved through the united efforts of the Abrahamic faiths.

## Our Values

*   **Compassion**: We care deeply about the suffering of others.
*   **Unity**: We are stronger together.
*   **Respect**: We honor the dignity of every person.
