# Annual Food Drive 2024

![Community Event](/images/community_event.png)

Our community came together in an incredible way for this year's Annual Food Drive. We set a goal to collect 3,000 lbs of food, and we smashed it by collecting over **5,000 lbs**!

Volunteers from all walks of life spent the day sorting, packing, and distributing food to local families in need.

### Highlights
- **500+** Families served
- **150** Volunteers participated
- **$2,000** Raised in additional donations

Thank you to everyone who made this possible!
