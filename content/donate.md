## Your Support Matters

Your donation helps us provide food, clean water, and other essentials to people in need. It also supports our programs that build bridges of understanding between Jews, Christians, and Muslims.

### Where Your Money Goes

*   **$25** provides food for a family for a week.
*   **$50** provides clean water for a village.
*   **$100** supports our childhood protection program.
