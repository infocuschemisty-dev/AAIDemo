## Get Involved

We rely on the dedication of volunteers to carry out our mission. Whether you can spare a few hours a month or want to take on a leadership role, there is a place for you at AAi.

### Volunteer Opportunities

*   **Food Distribution**: Help pack and distribute food to those in need.
*   **Event Planning**: Assist with organizing our interfaith events.
*   **Fundraising**: Help us raise the funds necessary to continue our work.
